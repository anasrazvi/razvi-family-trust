About the Trust

Razvi Family Trust (est. 2017) operates on the principle of Qarz‑e‑Hasanah—benevolent, interest‑free support—to help families overcome short‑term financial barriers with dignity and accountability.

Our Objectives

  • Provide interest‑free loans for marriage, education, sickness, and livelihood.
  • Offer need‑based Qarz‑e‑Hasanah after background verification.
  • Build a corpus for long‑term sustainability.

Who We Serve

Members and deserving families from Aal‑e‑Hasnain community groups (Razvi/Rizvi, Baig, Ansari, Haq) in and around Hyderabad.

Assistance is subject to verification and trustee approval.

Programs

Marriage Support

Interest‑free loans to meet essential marriage expenses with dignity.

Education Support

Fees, books, and skill training so students can continue learning.

Medical Support

Help during sickness and emergencies after due verification.

Livelihood Support

Tools, small capital, or bridge‑funding to restart income streams.
